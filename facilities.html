pool

Richard Hotels features a luxurious pool area designed for relaxation and rejuvenation. Dive into our crystal-clear swimming pool, which offers both a heated section for cooler days and an open section for sun-drenched afternoons. Lounge by the water with comfortable sunbeds, umbrellas, and towel service readily available. Our poolside bar offers refreshing drinks, cocktails, and light snacks, perfect for guests who wish to unwind in style.

Gym

Stay active in our state-of-the-art fitness center, fully equipped with a wide range of modern exercise equipment, including cardio machines, free weights, and strength-training stations. Open 24/7, the gym is available for guests of all fitness levels, whether you’re an avid fitness enthusiast or just looking for a quick workout. Personal trainers and complimentary workout classes are available to help you reach your fitness goals during your stay.

Spa

Indulge in the ultimate relaxation experience at our serene hotel spa. Our spa offers a variety of treatments, including massages, facials, body scrubs, and aromatherapy, each designed to refresh both body and mind. Guests can enjoy the tranquil atmosphere with soothing music, luxurious amenities, and highly trained therapists who tailor each treatment to individual needs. The spa also features a sauna and steam room, ideal for unwinding after a long day of travel or meetings.

Resto and Bar

Inviting space combines stylish decor with a welcoming atmosphere, making it the perfect spot for any occasion. With a diverse menu and carefully curated drinks, we aim to offer something for everyone. Join us for a memorable dining experience where great food, drinks, and ambiance come together.

Services

Taxi

For your convenience, we offer reliable taxi services to help you explore the city or reach your desired destination comfortably. Taxis are available right outside the hotel and can be easily arranged through our front desk.

Housekeeping

housekeeping plays a crucial role in ensuring guests have a clean, comfortable, and pleasant stay. Housekeepers are responsible for cleaning and preparing guest rooms, maintaining public areas, and sometimes assisting with laundry.
